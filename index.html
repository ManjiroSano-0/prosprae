How it works

Four steps from brief
to warm lead

01
Define your ideal buyer
We map your target profile together. Industry, company size, geography, job title. The more specific, the better the results.
02
Build a verified contact list
We source and verify decision-maker contacts using Apollo and AI enrichment. Every contact manually reviewed for relevance.
03
Run personalised outreach
A three-step email sequence goes out in your name. Personalised, plain text, written to feel like a genuine human reaching out.
04
Receive warm leads only
Positive replies are filtered and forwarded to you with full context. You step in only when someone has already said they are interested.
